OUTLOOK & RESILIENCE SCORE
Economic Outlook

The US economy demonstrates moderate resilience with stable growth and improving inflation dynamics, though fiscal and external imbalances pose medium-term challenges. Labor market cooling and persistent core inflation above target require careful monetary policy calibration.

Key Risks
  • Fiscal sustainability concerns with rising debt levels
  • External financing requirements from trade deficits
  • Potential monetary policy missteps
  • Geopolitical tensions and trade disruptions
